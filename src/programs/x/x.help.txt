x.js: Quick calculator.

Usage:
  x [args] [options]

Functions:
  abs, ceil, cos, date, days, floor,
  hash, hash64, ints, log, max, min,
  prod, rand, round, sign, sin, sqrt,
  sum, tan, years

Useful constants:
  π, pi, e, c, φ, phi

Scaling constants:
  f, p, n, μ, mu, m, k, M, G, B, T, P

Options:
  -s, --scientific,  -x, --hexadecimal,
  -d, --decimal,     -q, --quit,
  -h, --help
